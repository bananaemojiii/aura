About Aura Radio

We are an independent, non-profit, online radio station live streaming 24/7 from the heart of the city. Expect a continuous stream of the best and most varied music, carefully curated to bring you the sounds that inspire.

Our mission is to create a space where music lovers can discover new artists, genres, and sounds from around the globe. We believe in the power of music to connect communities and spark creativity.
